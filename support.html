Frequently Asked Questions
How do coins work?
Coins are spent during calls — 100 coins every 30 seconds. Buy more anytime from the Buy Coins screen.
How do I recharge my wallet?
Open Wallet from the bottom navigation and tap Recharge to choose an amount.
What happens if I run out of coins mid-call?
The call ends automatically and you'll be taken to the Recharge screen.
How do gifts work?
Gifts are separate from call earnings and convert into Diamonds for hosts.
